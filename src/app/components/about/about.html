About me

I’m a Mobile Developer with 5+ years of experience building scalable, high-performance mobile applications for iOS and Android. I specialize in delivering production-ready apps across fintech, banking, healthcare, and logistics — with a strong focus on performance, reliability, and seamless user experience.

Core Skills & Expertise - React Native & TypeScript :

  • Cross-platform mobile development: Expo & CLI
  • State Management: Redux Toolkit, Redux-Saga, React Query, RTK Query
  • UI/UX & Styling: Tailwind CSS, Styled Components, Responsive Design
  • Native Features: Native Bridging, Deep Linking, Push Notifications, Secure Storage, Biometric Authentication
  • Integrations: Firebase, Google Maps, Stripe Payments, GraphQL, REST APIs, WebSockets
  • In-App Purchases: Subscription and consumable product implementations for iOS & Android
  • Zoom SDK: Video conferencing and telemedicine integrations
  • Maps Integration: Google Maps, location tracking, geofencing
  • DevOps & CI/CD: Fastlane, App Center, CodePush, Firebase App Distribution
  • Testing & Performance: Jest, Detox, Flipper, Performance Optimization
  • Backend (basic): Node.js, Express.js, GraphQL, REST APIs, JWT, OAuth
  • Passionate about crafting secure, scalable, and user-friendly apps, bridging design and development to deliver smooth, impactful digital experiences.
